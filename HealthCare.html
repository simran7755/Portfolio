Health Care

  • Develped health management website for a business to allow patients to book appointments, doctors to view appointments, prescribe medicine and admin to manage both roles.

  • The programming tools that were used to make this website functional were PHP, HTML, MySQL and Javascript. The software tools that were used are XAMPP and Notepad++.

  • Business requirements were gathered and followed SDLC cycle to complete this project.
